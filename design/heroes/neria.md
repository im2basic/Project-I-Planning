## Passive — Tidal Wisdom

Whenever Neria applies a debuff, she gains 1 **Tide** stack (max 5).

Each stack grants:
- +5% Skill Damage
- +3% Accuracy

At max stacks: Neria's debuffs last +1 turn longer.

## Basic Attack — Aqua Bolt

- **Cooldown:** None
- Sends a condensed water sphere from the grimoire.
- **Effects:** Single Target Damage; 15% chance to apply **Slow** (1 turn).

## Skill 1 — Tidal Sigil

- **Cooldown:** 2 turns | **Multiplier:** 1.8× AoE
- Grimoire pages flutter outward forming ocean sigils beneath enemies.
- **Effects:** AoE Damage; applies **Slow** (2 turns); 25% chance per enemy for **Accuracy Down**.

## Skill 2 — Forbidden Tides

- **Cooldown:** 3 turns | **Multiplier:** 2.2× single-target
- Massive arcane water construct slams a single target.
- **Effects:** Heavy Single Target Damage; bonus +30% damage if target is Slowed OR Frostbite-d; generates 2 Tide stacks.

## Ultimate — Leviathan Archive

- **Cooldown:** 5 turns | **Multiplier:** 2.6× AoE
- Neria opens the grimoire fully, summoning the Leviathan's recorded memories.
- **Effects:**
  - Massive AoE Damage
  - Removes all enemy buffs
  - Applies **DEF Down** + **Slow** to all enemies
  - Bonus +20% damage per Tide stack at cast time

## Character Identity

**Strengths:** Powerful AoE damage; strong debuff control; pairs naturally with Glacier/Auron; scales well in long fights.

**Weaknesses:** Low HP/DEF; requires Tide stack setup; vulnerable to assassins; relies on debuffs.
