## Passive — Veteran's Resolve

Whenever Valdrik takes damage, gain 1 **Resolve** stack (max 5).

Each stack grants:
- +4% DEF
- +4% Shield Strength

At maximum stacks:
- Gains **Crowd Control Resistance** (immune to Freeze, Stun, Taunt)
- **Taunts applied by Valdrik cannot be removed** by enemy effects

## Basic Attack — Ashen Hammer

- **Cooldown:** None
- Valdrik swings his massive warhammer in a heavy arc infused with smoldering fire.
- **Effects:**
  - Single Target Damage
  - 20% chance to inflict **Burn** (2 turns)
  - **Generates 1 Resolve stack**

## Skill 1 — Blazing Bastion

- **Cooldown:** 2 turns | **Multiplier:** 1.5×
- Valdrik raises his tower shield and reinforces his allies with protective flames.
- **Effects:**
  - All allies gain **DEF Up** (+30%, 2 turns)
  - Grants a **Small Shield** to all allies based on Valdrik's HP (~10% of his max HP)

## Skill 2 — Scorchguard Slam

- **Cooldown:** 3 turns | **Multiplier:** 1.8× (AoE)
- Valdrik charges forward and slams his massive tower shield into the enemy formation.
- **Effects:**
  - AoE Damage
  - **50% chance to Taunt** all enemies (2 turns)
  - Applies **Burn** to all hit enemies
  - **Generates 1 Resolve stack**

## Ultimate — Inferno Fortress

- **Cooldown:** 5 turns | **Multiplier:** 2.6× (AoE damage component)
- Valdrik plants his tower shield into the battlefield and unleashes a blazing defensive fortress.
- **Effects:**
  - Massive **DEF Up** to all allies (+50%, 2 turns)
  - **Taunts all enemies** (2 turns)
  - Grants **Shield** to all allies based on Valdrik's HP (~25% of his max HP)
  - **Burns** all enemies (2 turns)

**Weaknesses:**
