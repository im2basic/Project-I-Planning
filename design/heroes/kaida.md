## Passive — Reaper's Dance

Whenever Kaida lands a Critical Hit, gain 1 **Infernal Momentum** stack (max 5).

Each stack grants:
- +4% Speed
- +4% Critical Damage

At max stacks: ignore 20% enemy DEF on all attacks.

## Basic Attack — Crimson Sever

- **Cooldown:** None
- Kaida darts forward with a rapid twin-sickle strike.
- **Effects:** Single Target Damage; 20% chance to inflict **Burn** (2 turns).

## Skill 1 — Chain Rend

- **Cooldown:** 2 turns | **Multiplier:** 1.5× single-target
- Kaida swings her chained sickles outward before pulling herself into a devastating follow-up.
- **Effects:** Single Target Damage; applies **Burn**; +25% Critical Hit Chance for this attack.

## Skill 2 — Phantom Pursuit

- **Cooldown:** 3 turns | **Multiplier:** 2.2× single-target
- Kaida vanishes into crimson smoke and reappears behind her target.
- **Effects:** Heavy Single Target Damage; +50% damage against Burned enemies; high Critical Damage scaling.

## Ultimate — Infernal Reaping

- **Cooldown:** 5 turns | **Multiplier:** 3.5× single-target
- Kaida unleashes a relentless storm of chained sickle attacks ending in an execution strike.
- **Effects:** Massive Single Target Damage; applies **Burn**; +20% damage per active Burn on target (max +60%); ignores 30% enemy DEF.

## Character Identity

**Strengths:** Fastest Fire hero; excellent boss killer; strong crit scaling; high burst damage; exceptional mobility.

**Weaknesses:** Low HP/DEF; limited AoE; vulnerable to crowd control; requires setup for maximum damage.

## Chibi Animations

- **Idle:** spins one sickle around finger, chains sway, small embers drift
- **Attack:** quick cross-slash → chain whip follow-up → assassin stance
- **Skill 1:** sickles swing outward → chains wrap target area → rapid spinning slash
- **Skill 2:** dissolves into crimson smoke → reappears behind target → fast execution strike
- **Ultimate:** chains erupt outward → multiple infernal slashes → crimson reaper silhouette → massive finishing cross-cut
- **Death:** sickles fall from hands → chains collapse → embers fade → falls to one knee
