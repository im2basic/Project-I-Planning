## Passive — Song of Serenity

Whenever Marina uses a healing skill, all healed allies gain **Serenity** (max 3 stacks).

Serenity grants:
- +5% Healing Received
- +5% DEF
- Duration: 2 turns

At max stacks: removes one debuff from the ally.

## Basic Attack — Aqua Melody

- **Cooldown:** None
- Plucks a magical harp string, sending a condensed water wave at the target.
- **Effects:** Single Target Damage; small chance to reduce enemy **ATK** (-15%, 1 turn).

## Skill 1 — Healing Symphony

- **Cooldown:** 2 turns | **Heal multiplier:** 0.6× ATK (large team heal)
- Marina performs a soothing melody that restores all allies.
- **Effects:** **AoE Heal**; removes 1 debuff from all allies; applies Serenity.

## Skill 2 — Tidal Harmony

- **Cooldown:** 3 turns | **Multiplier:** 0.4× ATK (heal + shield)
- Harp resonates with ocean blessing, surrounding allies with protective waters.
- **Effects:** Grants **Shield** to all allies (15% of Marina's max HP); **DEF Up** (+30%, 2 turns); applies Serenity.

## Ultimate — Ocean's Requiem

- **Cooldown:** 5 turns | **Heal multiplier:** 1.2× ATK
- Marina performs a divine symphony calling forth the ancient sea spirits.
- **Effects:**
  - Massive **Team Heal**
  - **Cleanses** all debuffs from all allies
  - Increases **Healing Received** (+30%, 2 turns)
- <mark>**Source PDF cleanup:** The original sheet listed "Restores a small amount of Energy" as a 4th effect. Since the Energy system was deleted (audit Q4), that effect is simply removed — no replacement. Marina's Ultimate is a pure heal / cleanse / healing-amplification tool.</mark>

## Character Identity

**Strengths:** Exceptional healing; strong team sustain; reliable cleansing; defensive utility; excellent support scaling.

**Weaknesses:** Low damage; limited offensive presence; vulnerable if focused; team-dependent.

## Chibi Animations

- **Idle:** Oceanheart Harp floating beside her, water notes drift upward, small bubbles around her
- **Attack:** plucks harp string → water projectile launches → harp remains visible
- **Skill 1:** gentle melody plays → healing waves spread → water lilies briefly appear
- **Skill 2:** protective water rings form → ocean sigils rotate around allies → harp glows
- **Ultimate:** Oceanheart Harp floats before her → grand symphony → water lilies bloom across battlefield → ocean spirits swirl around allies → healing notes fill the air

## Team Role

Primary sustain Support. Best paired with fragile DPS lineups (assassins, mages) who need her cleanse to survive heavy debuff content.

## Recommended Partners

- **Glacier** — Marina sustains while Frostguard builds
- **Neria** — Neria's debuffs are cleansed off the team by Marina
- **Auron** — fragile back-line that Marina keeps alive
- **Kaida / Kael** — assassins thrive with her sustain
