## Passive — Crimson Dominion

Whenever Emberia applies **Burn** to an enemy, gain 1 **Crimson Flame** stack (max 5).

Each stack grants:
- +5% ATK
- +3% Burn damage dealt

At max stacks: Burn effects ignore 20% enemy DEF.

## Basic Attack — Ember Bolt

- **Cooldown:** None
- Emberia launches a concentrated sphere of magical fire.
- **Effects:** Single Target Damage; 30% chance to inflict **Burn** (2 turns).

## Skill 1 — Scarlet Nova

- **Cooldown:** 2 turns | **Multiplier:** 1.8× (AoE)
- Emberia conjures a blazing crimson explosion beneath all enemies.
- **Effects:** AoE Damage; applies **Burn** (2 turns); 25% chance per enemy for **ATK Down**.

## Skill 2 — Infernal Bloom

- **Cooldown:** 3 turns | **Multiplier:** 1.8× (AoE)
- Crimson flames erupt across the battlefield in the shape of blooming fire flowers.
- **Effects:** AoE Damage; extends existing **Burn** duration by 1 turn; bonus damage to Burned enemies (+30%).

## Ultimate — Crimson Cataclysm

- **Cooldown:** 5 turns | **Multiplier:** 2.6× (AoE)
- A colossal storm of magical fire engulfs the battlefield.
- **Effects:** Massive AoE Damage; applies **Burn** to all enemies; bonus damage for each active Burn effect (+15% per Burn stack across team).

## Character Identity

**Strengths:** Highest AoE damage in Fire faction; exceptional Burn scaling; excellent wave clear; powerful against large groups.

**Weaknesses:** Low HP/DEF; vulnerable to assassins; relies on Burn setup; limited defensive utility.
