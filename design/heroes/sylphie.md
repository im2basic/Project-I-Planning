## Passive — Spirit's Embrace

Whenever an ally falls below 50% HP:
- Restore a small amount of HP (10% of Sylphie's ATK)
- Remove 1 debuff
- Sylphie gains 1 **Nature Blessing** stack (max 5)

Each stack grants:
- +3% Healing Done

## Basic Attack — Verdant Bolt

- **Cooldown:** None
- Sylphie launches a concentrated wind blossom at an enemy.
- **Effects:** Single Target Damage; small chance to reduce enemy Accuracy (-15%, 1 turn).

## Skill 1 — Nature's Embrace

- **Cooldown:** 2 turns | **Heal multiplier:** 0.5× ATK
- Sylphie summons healing winds around her allies.
- **Effects:** **Heal All Allies**; removes 1 debuff; small **Heal Over Time** (2 turns).

## Skill 2 — Zephyr's Renewal

- **Cooldown:** 3 turns | **Multiplier:** 1.0× ATK (heal + buff)
- A powerful breeze strengthens the entire team.
- **Effects:** **ATK Up** (+30%); **Speed Up** (+30%); **Healing Received Up** (+25%); lasts 2 turns.

## Ultimate — Sanctuary of the World Tree

- **Cooldown:** 5 turns | **Heal multiplier:** 1.3× ATK
- Calls forth the spirit of the World Tree.
- **Effects:**
  - Massive **Team Heal**
  - **Removes all debuffs**
  - Grants **Regeneration** (2 turns)
  - Grants **Damage Reduction** (+30%, 2 turns)

## Character Identity

**Strengths:** Strongest healer at launch; team-wide cleansing; excellent sustain; powerful buffs; easy to fit into any team.

**Weaknesses:** Very low damage; fragile defense; vulnerable to burst; relies on allies for kills.

## Team Role

Primary Support across nearly every Wind team. Strong universal sustainer — fits even mono-Fire or mono-Water teams when needed.

## Recommended Partners

- **Lyria / Kael** — Wind core team
- **Valdrik / Glacier** — sustains them through tough fights
- **Emberia** — back-line healing while she nukes
