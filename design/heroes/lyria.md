## Passive — Huntress's Instinct

Whenever Lyria lands a Critical Hit, she gains 1 **Focus** stack (max 5).

Each stack grants:
- +4% Crit Damage
- +4% Accuracy

At max stacks: ignore 25% enemy DEF.

## Basic Attack — Zephyr Shot

- **Cooldown:** None
- Lyria fires a wind-infused arrow with deadly precision.
- **Effects:** Single Target Damage; +15% Critical Rate on this attack.

## Skill 1 — Gale Piercer

- **Cooldown:** 2 turns | **Multiplier:** 1.8× single-target
- Draws back her bow and releases a concentrated wind arrow.
- **Effects:** Heavy Single Target Damage; **ignores 30% DEF**; +20% Critical Chance on this attack.

## Skill 2 — Tempest Volley

- **Cooldown:** 3 turns | **Multiplier:** 0.55× per hit × 5 hits = 2.75× total
- Leaps backward while unleashing a flurry of enchanted arrows.
- **Effects:** Hits 5 random enemies (or same target if only 1 alive); each hit may crit independently; **grants 1 Focus stack** (one total per cast, not per crit).

## Ultimate — Skyfall Barrage

- **Cooldown:** 5 turns | **Multiplier:** 3.5× single-target (plus splash)
- Calls upon the spirits of the wind to rain countless arrows.
- **Effects:**
  - Massive Single Target Damage
  - Additional 0.5× Splash Damage to adjacent enemies
  - **Consumes Focus Stacks** for bonus damage (+30% per stack consumed)
  - **Guaranteed Critical Hit**

## Character Identity

**Strengths:** Highest Crit Rate at launch; excellent boss killer; exceptional accuracy; strong burst damage; reliable DPS.

**Weaknesses:** Fragile; limited crowd control; requires protection; focuses on single targets.
