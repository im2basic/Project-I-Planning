## Passive — Frozen Bulwark

Whenever Glacier takes damage, gain 1 **Frostguard** stack (max 5).

Each stack grants:
- +4% DEF
- (Implicit) +3% damage absorption built into the DEF formula

At max stacks: enemies that attack Glacier have a **25% chance to become Frozen** (1 turn).

## Basic Attack — Winterfang Thrust

- **Cooldown:** None
- Glacier drives his gunlance forward, releasing freezing energy on impact.
- **Effects:** Single Target Damage; 15% chance to inflict **Frostbite** (-20% Speed, -10% DEF, 2 turns).

## Skill 1 — Cryo Bastion

- **Cooldown:** 2 turns | **Multiplier:** 1.0× (defensive)
- Plants Winterfang into the ground and creates a frost barrier.
- **Effects:** Grants **Shield** to self equal to 30% of max HP; **DEF Up** (+30%) self; applies 1 Frostguard stack.

## Skill 2 — Avalanche Shell

- **Cooldown:** 3 turns | **Multiplier:** 1.8× AoE
- Fires a cryogenic shell that explodes on impact.
- **Effects:** AoE Damage; applies **Frostbite**; 30% chance per enemy to **Slow** (1 turn).

## Ultimate — Eternal Winter

- **Cooldown:** 5 turns | **Multiplier:** 2.6× AoE
- Glacier fires a glacier cannon blast that erupts into a catastrophic blizzard.
- **Effects:** Massive AoE Damage; applies **Frostbite** to all enemies; 40% chance per enemy to **Freeze** (1 turn); grants maximum Frostguard stacks to self.

## Character Identity

**Strengths:** Extremely durable; excellent frontline presence; powerful crowd control; strong team utility; reliable Frost cycling.

**Weaknesses:** Slow turn cadence; lower critical potential; needs frontline positioning; limited burst damage.

## Chibi Animations

- **Idle:** Winterfang planted beside him, frost mist drifting, snowflakes around feet
- **Attack:** heavy lance thrust → small cryogenic shell fires
- **Skill 1:** plants gunlance → ice barrier erupts upward → frost aura forms
- **Skill 2:** gunlance charges blue → fires glacier shell → ice explosion
- **Ultimate:** raises Winterfang skyward → massive frost cannon → glacier beam fires → blizzard engulfs → ice crystals erupt from ground
- **Death:** drops to one knee → Winterfang falls → frost mist fades → collapses on frozen ground

## Team Role

Alternative Tank to Valdrik. Where Valdrik is pure defense + Taunt, Glacier brings frontline AoE + crowd control. Pairs well with crit DPS who exploit Frozen targets.

## Recommended Partners

- **Auron** — Frozen enemies take massive damage from Auron's Frostbound abilities
- **Neria** — extends Glacier's control with Slow/Freeze
- **Marina** — keeps him sustained as Frostguard builds
- **Kaida** — exploits debuffed enemies
