## Passive — Stormfang's Eye

Whenever Kael lands a Critical Hit, gain 1 **Predator** stack (max 5).

Each stack grants:
- +4% Critical Damage
- +4% Speed

At max stacks: ignore 25% enemy DEF.

## Basic Attack — Tiger Fang

- **Cooldown:** None
- Quick twin-blade slash with wind claw projectile.
- **Effects:** Single Target Damage; +15% Critical Rate on this attack.

## Skill 1 — Emerald Pounce

- **Cooldown:** 2 turns | **Multiplier:** 1.5× single-target
- Spectral Stormfang Tiger lunges alongside Kael.
- **Effects:** Heavy Single Target Damage; +25% Critical Chance; ignores 20% DEF.

## Skill 2 — Phantom Hunt

- **Cooldown:** 3 turns | **Multiplier:** 0.55× per hit × 4 hits = 2.2× total
- Kael disappears into the wind and strikes from multiple angles.
- **Effects:** Hits 4 times on selected target; each hit can crit; generates 1 Predator stack per crit.

## Ultimate — Stormfang Execution

- **Cooldown:** 5 turns | **Multiplier:** 3.5× single-target
- Massive spectral wind tiger emerges from a cyclone and tears through.
- **Effects:**
  - Massive Single Target Damage
  - **Guaranteed Critical Hit**
  - Consumes Predator stacks for bonus damage (+30% per stack consumed)
  - Ignores 30% enemy DEF

## Character Identity

**Strengths:** Extremely high burst damage; exceptional mobility; strong boss killer; excellent crit scaling; fastest Wind assassin.

**Weaknesses:** Fragile; requires setup; limited team utility; vulnerable if focused.

## Chibi Animations

- **Idle:** twin blades drawn, wind swirls around him, spectral tiger eyes appear briefly
- **Attack:** quick slash → wind claw launches rightward
- **Skill 1:** Stormfang Tiger appears → tiger lunges rightward
- **Skill 2:** dash afterimages → multiple strikes rightward
- **Ultimate:** massive cyclone forms → Stormfang Tiger emerges → tiger charges → wind explosion
- **Death:** blades remain visible briefly → falls to one knee → collapses as wind disperses
