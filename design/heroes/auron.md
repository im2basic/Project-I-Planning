**Visual identity note:** Auron's design uses ice/frost imagery (Frostbound Bracer, Frost Charges, Freeze status). Mechanically he is **Water-element** — there is no separate Ice element. Frost/Freeze are Water-flavored status effects.

## Passive — Cryogenic Resonance

Whenever Auron lands a Critical Hit, gain 1 **Frost Charge** stack (max 5).

Each stack grants:
- +5% Critical Damage

At max stacks: Auron's abilities ignore 20% enemy DEF.

## Basic Attack — Frost Shard

- **Cooldown:** None
- The Frostbound Bracer creates a razor-sharp ice crystal and launches it at the target.
- **Effects:** Single Target Damage; 10% chance to inflict **Freeze** (1 turn).

## Skill 1 — Glacial Lance

- **Cooldown:** 2 turns | **Multiplier:** 1.8× single-target
- Condenses cryogenic energy into a massive ice spear.
- **Effects:** High Single Target Damage; guaranteed hit (Accuracy auto-passes); +25% Critical Chance.

## Skill 2 — Crystal Bloom

- **Cooldown:** 3 turns | **Multiplier:** 0.55× per hit × 4 hits = 2.2× total
- The bracer summons multiple floating frost crystals that fire rapidly.
- **Effects:** Hits 4 times on selected target; each hit can crit; 20% chance per hit to **Freeze**; generates 1 Frost Charge per crit.

## Ultimate — Absolute Zero Domain

- **Cooldown:** 5 turns | **Multiplier:** 3.5× single-target
- Auron releases the full power of the Frostbound Bracer.
- **Effects:**
  - Massive Single Target Damage
  - Ignores 30% enemy DEF
  - **Guaranteed Freeze** (1 turn)
  - Bonus damage against Frozen enemies (+30%)
  - Consumes Frost Charges for bonus damage (+25% per stack consumed)

## Character Identity

**Strengths:** Highest Accuracy in Water roster; strong single-target damage; freeze specialist; excellent boss killer; reliable crits; strong scaling through Frost Charges.

**Weaknesses:** Low survivability; limited AoE; requires setup; no team utility.
